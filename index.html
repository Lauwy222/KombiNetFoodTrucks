Services

Mission Statement

Our mission is to preserve the classic beauty of Volkswagen T1 and T2 vans while offering an innovative solution for the food and beverage industry that reduces carbon emissions, meets future regulatory standards, and delivers a unique and nostalgic experience for customers.

DETAILS
Services

Custom VW Food Trucks

We restore and convert original Volkswagen T1 and T2 vans into fully functional food trucks—tailored to your menu, workflow, and branding. From coffee and cocktail bars to street food and ice cream, we design layouts that work for you.

DETAILS
Services

Turnkey or Shell Options

Choose between a fully restored VW van ready for your own fit-out, or a complete turnkey build with appliances installed and ready to serve. We’ll match the build level to your budget and goals.

DETAILS
Services

Electric Conversions

Future-proof your food truck with a full electric drivetrain. With ranges up to 300 km, our EV builds help you meet city emission rules while sending a strong sustainability message with your brand.
